Altitudes

Output altitudes are absolute MSL = ground elevation + AGL. Min/Max altitude is restricted to 10 m increments. Descent uses the same step as ascent but offset by half a step, so descent altitudes fall between ascent altitudes (e.g. up 10/20/30…/60 → down 55/45/35/…).
